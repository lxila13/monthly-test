Mini Olympics

Our July competition will be a mini olympics. We will have many different events
  • Athletics
  • Karate
  • Basketball
  • Football
  • and many more
The event will take place the last week in July in London. It is open to all schools in the UK. Schools will need to register by 31 March at the latest. Schools will receive confirmation and a welcome pack by 04 April. Schools will receive their entrants list by 10 April.

Last Year's Competition

Who can forget the thrills and excitement of last year's mini olympics? Certainly not us. The event kicked off with a grand opening ceremony, which included live music and action. All of the schools marched around the track in their teams with their banners. The torch was lit and the olympics were declared open. The atmosphere was electrifying. Can we beat it this year? Oh yes we can. This year the live music will be provided by none other than the well-known JK Band. The opening ceremony will be 2 hours long and the torch bearer will be selected at random from the entrants. The closing ceremony will take place on day 7. This will include the presentation of all medals, live music and dance routines.
We look forward to welcoming you all again.
